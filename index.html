About Me

I’m Jennie, a bilingual (German/English) social media manager, copywriter, and creative strategist driven by a lifelong fascination with the unique narratives of brands, both large and small. With a background in fiction writing and a Master's in education focusing on English and German literature, I bring a narrative-centric approach to crafting unique brand stories on social media. From my early years, I've immersed myself in fiction writing, a background that shapes my approach to the worlds of social media strategy and content creation.

Having worked in education and journalism, I found my passion lies in developing stories for real brands. Now, I'm excited to support small businesses and entrepreneurs, aiming to foster growth, establish positive brand presence, and build authentic online communities. Join me on this journey where storytelling and strategic planning elevate brands digitally, leaving a lasting impression.
